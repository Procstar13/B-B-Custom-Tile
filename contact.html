Frequently Asked Questions

How much does tile installation cost?

Tile installation costs vary based on the type of tile, project size, and complexity. We provide free, detailed estimates for all projects. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How long does a typical installation take?

Most bathroom projects take 2-3 days, while kitchen backsplashes can be completed in 1 day. Larger projects may take a week or more. We'll provide a timeline during your consultation.

Do you provide materials or do I buy them?

We can work either way! We can source materials for you (often at better prices) or work with materials you've already purchased. We'll discuss options during your consultation.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes! We're fully licensed (#12345), insured, and bonded. We're also BBB accredited with an A+ rating. Your project is protected.
