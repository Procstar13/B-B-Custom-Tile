About B&B Custom Tile

Your Trusted Tile Installation Experts

Founded in 2008 by brothers Brian and Brad Thompson, B&B Custom Tile began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide exceptional tile installation services that transform homes across Arizona.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into Arizona's premier tile installation company, serving thousands of satisfied customers throughout the Phoenix metropolitan area and beyond.

Our commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as the go-to choice for residential and commercial tile projects.
